BESPOKE DIAMOND JEWELLERY

Divulge in the opulence of Admire Diamonds. We create exceptionally crafted bespoke jewellery, tailored exactly to your desires.

Procedure

Our simple 3 step process designed to create the diamond that you've always dreamed about.

Consult

Book a personal consultation at your convenience with one of our expert team members. We will guide you through the process of your bespoke design by discussing budget, desired specifications and we provide advice to help you confidently choose the perfect stone for your design and get the most value for your money.

Design

After your consultation, our design team will create a visualization for you to experience your design. We're here to offer advice and guidance on aesthetics, metal selection and profiles to ensure a stunning and elegant finish. If you have any changes in mind, we'll be happy to update your design. Once you're fully satisfied with your custom design, we'll send it for production.

Manufacture

Once your design is approved, it will be sent to our team of expert artisans to bring your vision to life in our workshop. Using a combination of traditional techniques honed over generations and cutting-edge technology, these skilled craftsmen will handcraft your piece to meet your exact specifications. We'll notify you when your unique item is ready with the option of delivery or in-store pickup based on your preference.

Results

The process takes between 4 to 6 weeks in total from confirmation of order to completion. The completed dream piece will be delivery along with all necessary certifications and appraisals (you will need this for insurance purposes). We offer nationwide, fully insured shipping methods or, in some cases, hand delivery to you.
